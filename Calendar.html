Events Calendar

Recurring Yearly Major Events

  • Winter Camp (January) — Klondike
  • Spring Camporee — Scout-skills competition based weekend campout (usually in the Spring)
  • Summer Camp (July or August) — Varies between different BSA camps in Washington (and occasionally Oregon)
  • Summer High Adventure — Philmont — Every 2–3 years, older Scouts can participate

Courts of Honor

Every quarter, there is a Court of Honor to recognize Scouts that have advanced in rank and/or earned Merit Badges. It's a fun event where the Scouting family comes together for a potluck dinner, an awards ceremony, and skits performed by the Scouts.

Other Annual Events

  • Merit Badge Fairs — Scouts can participate in fairs hosted by the Lake Shores District and neighboring districts to earn Merit Badges
  • Training and Scout Fairs — Multiple opportunities throughout the year
  • Fundraising Projects — Scouts plan and execute fundraising activities to support the Troop
  • Community Service Projects — Food collection, trash sweeps, and other community activities
  • Shooting sports weekends — Scouts can try and earn NRA qualifications, merit badges, and experience shooting a variety of guns
