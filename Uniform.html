Uniform Guide

A typical Scouts BSA uniform includes these essentials:

Official Tan BSA Shirt

Official BSA class A uniform, required for all events, and to be worn to Troop meetings. Click here to buy it.

Green Pants

Any green pants are acceptable

Belt

Official BSA belt, ussually only worn for ceremonies/special occasions

Neckerchief

Official BSA neckerchief of our troop, also only worn for special occasion/ceremonies

Class B

During warmer months Scouts may wear official Class B shirts (camp shirts / troop shirts).
