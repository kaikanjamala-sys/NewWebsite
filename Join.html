How to Join

Thank you for your interest in Scouts BSA Troops 641 and 8641! Both Troops are part of the Lake Shores District in the Chief Seattle Council of Boy Scouts of America.

If you are interested in learning more, please fill out the interest form for:

  • BSA Troop 641

Online Application

To fill out an online application, go to my.scouting.org and create an account. You will then be able to complete an online application.

my.Scouting.org is also a helpful resource that parents and guardians will use throughout their Scout's time in BSA.

Just Come to a Meeting!

You're also welcome to just show up to a Monday meeting at 7:00 PM at St. Louise Parish, 141 156th Ave SE, Bellevue, WA 98007. We'd love to walk you through the sign-up process!
