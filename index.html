About Us:

Our troop

Troops 641 and 8641, are part of the Lake Shores District in the Chief Seattle Council of the Boy Scouts of America (BSA).

We are based in Bellevue WA, and we meet every monday from 7:00-8:30 PM at St. Louise parish

Our Mission

Our mission is to: Develop youth to become outstanding citizens through activities that help them grow their practical knowledge, leadership skills, and self-reliance.

Our Scouting Family

Our sponsor organization, St. Louise Parish also sponsors Cub Scout Pack 641, which feeds into our troop. We are a family troop, so we accept both boys and girls! We do all of our activities together, including camping trips, service projects, and more. We also have a very big Order of the Arrow (OA) presence, with many of our scouts being members of the OA.

Interested in joining our Scouting Family?

Volunteer Opportunities

Part of our mission is to serve and give back to the community. As said in the scout oath, "A scout is helpful". Therefore, we have multiple volunteer opportunities throughout the year, including:

  • Pancake Breakfasts where we feed the community
  • Flag retirement ceremonies
  • Helping at the St. Louise parish picnic
  • Trail maintenence

Contact & Questions

For more information, come to a Monday meeting at 7:00 PM at St. Louise Parish, 141 156th Ave SE, Bellevue, WA 98007.

You can also start an online application or create an account at my.scouting.org.
